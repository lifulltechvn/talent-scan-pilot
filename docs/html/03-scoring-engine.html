LLM Score (30% of Final)

Claude Haiku nhận job requirements + candidate structured_data (ẩn PII) → score 0–100 + reasoning. AI đánh giá: career trajectory, domain relevance, soft indicators. Cost ~$0.002/call. Retry 3× nếu throttled.
